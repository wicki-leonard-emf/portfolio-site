# Branding Notes  

## Color Palette  
- **Primary**: `#0E7490` – A rich blue tone for highlights and key components.  
- **Secondary**: `#1E3A8A` – A dark blue for backgrounds and headers.  
- **Accent**: `#D97706` – A warm orange accent color for buttons and interactive elements.  
- **Background**: `#F9FAFB` – A very light grey for page backgrounds to maintain contrast.  
- **Text**: `#0F172A` – A dark grey for readable body text.  

## Typography  
- **Primary Font**: Inter, sans‑serif for headings and body text.  
- **Secondary Font**: Arial, sans‑serif as a fallback.  
- **Body Text Size**: 16 px for comfortable reading on web.  
- **Heading Sizes**:  
  - H1: 36 px  
  - H2: 30 px  
  - H3: 24 px  

## Brand Tone  
- Professional and modern with a friendly touch.  
- Clear and concise messaging.  
- Focus on showcasing skills and projects with authenticity and confidence.  

These notes establish an initial visual and verbal identity for the portfolio. Feel free to iterate on colors, typography, and tone as the design evolves.
